Discover Melbourne

Melbourne is famous for its artistic laneways, rich coffee culture, historic trams, shopping streets and Federation Square.

By Melbpal - Own work, CC BY-SA 4.0

Visitors enjoy shopping, museums, street art, botanical gardens, sporting events and riverside dining experiences.

The city is also known for its multicultural food scene, rooftop cafés, famous cricket grounds and beautiful architecture.

Tourist Tips

Visit Federation Square, St Kilda Beach, Queen Victoria Market and the Royal Botanic Gardens.

Best season to visit: Autumn and spring.

Must Try

  • Laneway cafés
  • Tram rides
  • Street art tours
  • Yarra River walk
